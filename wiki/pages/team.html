Dr. Preeti Srivastava

Primary investigator

Dr. Preeti Srivastava is a professor at IIT Delhi and the project supervisor for iGEM-IIT Delhi. She has completed her post-doctoral research studies from the National Cancer Institute, Maryland, USA, with her research interests lying in the fields of Molecular Biology and Genetics. Prof. Preeti has guided the team from the start, right from the ideation of the project to its execution. She has been the constant pillar of support through the course of the project and has always provided valuable insights into the experimental workflow and troubleshooting drawn from her experiences.

Dr. D. Sundar

Secondary investigator

Dr. D. Sundar is the Institute Chair Professor at IIT Delhi. He has completed his post-doctoral research from John Hopkins University, Maryland, USA, with his research interests lying in the field of Bioinformatics and Computational Biology. Dr. Sundar has conducted extensive research in the fields of genome editing tool designing, biological studies of natural drugs, and metabolic engineering, reporting impactful insights, and accomplishing multiple awards and accolades for the same. Prof. Sundar has always been a stalwart support for the team, helping them out at every step, and helping them tackle obstacles, always guiding the team towards the right path.
